Chamber of Commerce Site Plan

Site Name

Tarija City Chamber

The city of Tarija is often called beautiful city, which sounds well with Chamber added at the end of it. The website URL will be www.beautifultarijachamber.bo.

Site Purpose

The purpose of the Tarija City Chamber website is to create a community and networking opportunities for businesses. Local businesses of Tarija, Bolivia, big and small, will be able to connect and communicate together using the resources provided by the website.

Another purpose of the website is to inform members and visitors of local events and activities regarding Tarija, Bolivia. There will be spotlight businesses which will be featured on the website which will help bring exposure to participating companies.

Scenarios

What businesses are participating in the Sandy City Chamber?

Should my business join the Chamber of Commerce in the area?

What upcoming events can my business participate in?

How can I contact and learn more about local businesses?

Color Schema

The primary color is a dark red which will be featured on each page's header or footer.

#000000

The secondary color is a black which will be used on titles and text on white or dark red backgrounds and on the navigation menu.

#ffffff

This accent color is a white which will may be used much on white or backgrounds and texts.

#bbb

This accent color is a gray which will be an alternative to gray backgrounds.
